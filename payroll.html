Payroll Standard Operating Procedure

Purpose

The purpose of this SOP is to ensure all employees are paid accurately and on time, in compliance with labor laws, and to maintain accurate payroll records for auditing purposes.

Scope

This procedure applies to all employees of the company, including full-time, part-time, and contract workers.

Responsibilities

HR Department: Maintains employee records, collects and verifies timesheets.
Finance Department: Calculates pay, deducts appropriate taxes, processes payroll payments, and records payroll transactions.

Procedure

  1. HR collects timesheets and any other necessary documentation for the payroll period.
  2. Verify the accuracy of the timesheets and approvals.
  3. Finance calculates gross pay based on hours worked and any other compensation (overtime, bonuses).
  4. Deduct federal, state, and any other applicable taxes along with benefits and other deductions.
  5. Process payroll payments via direct deposit or check on the scheduled payday.
  6. Distribute pay stubs to employees, either electronically or in paper form.
  7. Record all payroll transactions in the accounting software for financial reporting and compliance.

Documentation

Maintain detailed records of timesheets, payroll calculations, tax filings, and pay stubs for at least seven years to comply with auditing requirements and labor laws.

Review and Update

This SOP should be reviewed annually to ensure compliance with current labor laws and internal policies. Adjustments should be made as necessary to reflect changes in regulations or company procedures.
