Purchasing Standard Operating Procedure

Purpose

The purpose of this SOP is to ensure that all purchases made on behalf of the company are necessary, justified, and provide the best possible value to the company.

Scope

This procedure applies to all purchases of goods and services required for the operation of the company.

Responsibilities

Department Heads: Responsible for identifying needs and initiating purchase requests.
Purchasing Department: Validates purchase requests, selects suppliers, negotiates terms, and processes orders.
Finance Department: Reviews and approves expenditures, processes payments.

Procedure

  1. Identify the need for goods or services and complete a purchase request form, including a detailed description and justification for the purchase.
  2. Submit the purchase request form to the Purchasing Department for review and approval.
  3. The Purchasing Department selects suitable suppliers, obtains quotes, and negotiates terms and prices.
  4. Once a supplier is selected, the Purchasing Department issues a Purchase Order (PO), referencing the approved purchase request.
  5. The supplier delivers the goods or services, and the receiving department confirms the order's accuracy and condition.
  6. The invoice from the supplier is matched with the PO and receiving documents, approved, and forwarded to the Finance Department for payment.

Documentation

All purchase requests, POs, delivery receipts, and invoices must be properly documented and filed. Documentation should be retained for a minimum of seven years for auditing and compliance purposes.

Review and Update

This SOP should be reviewed annually to ensure that it reflects current best practices, market conditions, and compliance with any new regulations. Changes should be documented and communicated to all relevant staff members.
