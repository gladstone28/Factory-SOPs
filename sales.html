Sales Standard Operating Procedure

Purpose

The purpose of this SOP is to ensure all sales are processed in a timely, accurate, and properly documented manner, enhancing customer satisfaction and reliability in our sales operations.

Scope

This procedure applies to all sales transactions processed by the company.

Responsibilities

Sales Team: Receives orders, confirms inventory availability, and communicates with customers.
Shipping Department: Prepares and dispatches orders.
Finance Department: Invoices customers and records sales transactions.

Procedure

  1. The sales team receives an order via email, phone, or the online order system.
  2. Check inventory for product availability. If the product is not available, inform the customer of the expected restock date.
  3. Confirm the order with the customer, including price, delivery date, and payment terms.
  4. Prepare a sales order and forward it to the Shipping Department.
  5. The Shipping Department prepares the order, dispatches it, and updates the sales order with the tracking information.
  6. The Finance Department issues an invoice to the customer and records the sale.

Documentation

Keep all sales orders, customer communications, shipping documents, and invoices for a minimum of five years for auditing and customer service purposes.

Review and Update

This SOP should be reviewed annually and updated as needed to reflect changes in product offerings, customer service policies, or regulatory requirements.
